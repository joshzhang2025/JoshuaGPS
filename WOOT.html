Woot 是什么What is Woot?

先理解 Woot 平台本身，以及 Frual 在 Woot / Amazon Deal 业务链路中的定位。Start with what Woot is and where Frual fits in the Woot / Amazon Deal service chain.

1

亚马逊全资子公司A wholly owned Amazon subsidiary

  • 2010年6月，亚马逊以1.1亿美元现金完成了对 Woot 的全资收购。In June 2010, Amazon completed its full cash acquisition of Woot for USD 110 million.
2

美国知名团购网站A well-known U.S. deal site

  • www.woot.com 是美国著名的团购网站，月活跃用户超过千万。www.woot.com is a well-known U.S. group-buying and deal site with more than 10 million monthly active users.
3

Amazon 高级卖家High-weight Amazon seller account

  • Woot 在亚马逊美国站拥有高权重 VC 账号。Woot has a high-weight Vendor Central account on Amazon US.
  • 可通过亚马逊内部数据联通，为其他卖家提供多元营销推广服务。Through Amazon-side data and process connectivity, it can support diversified marketing services for other sellers.
4

Frual 在 Woot 业务中的定位Frual’s role in the Woot business

  • Frual 帮助 Amazon 第三方卖家通过 Woot / Amazon Deal 渠道做促销销售。Frual helps Amazon third-party sellers run promotional sales through Woot / Amazon Deal channels.
  • Frual 不持货、不发货、不取得产品所有权。Frual does not hold inventory, ship goods, or take product ownership.
  • Frual 的核心角色是服务方、流程推进方、资金与规则中枢。Frual acts as a service provider, process coordinator, and hub for funding and rules.
👥

业务中的核心角色Core Roles in the Business

这条链路由卖家、Frual、Woot 和 Amazon 共同组成。The business chain is formed by sellers, Frual, Woot, and Amazon.

1

卖家Seller

  • 产品所有人，Amazon 店铺实际经营者。Owns the products and operates the Amazon store.
  • 提供产品、库存、价格、资料。Provides product information, inventory, price, and required materials.
  • 承担产品质量、合规、物流等责任。Takes responsibility for product quality, compliance, and logistics.
2

FrualFrual

  • Woot 官方 Vendor，帮助卖家对接 Woot。An official Woot vendor that helps sellers connect with Woot.
  • 推进报价、提交、活动、广告、退库存、结款等流程。Drives quotation, submission, deal execution, ads, inventory return, and settlement workflows.
  • 核心是服务、流程、资源整合和风险控制。Focuses on service delivery, workflow coordination, resource integration, and risk control.
3

WootWoot

  • Amazon 旗下 Deal / 促销平台。A deal and promotion platform under Amazon.
  • 审核产品和价格，执行 Deal 活动。Reviews products and prices and executes deal events.
  • 处理划库存、退库存、结款等流程。Handles inventory transfer, returns, and settlement-related processes.
4

AmazonAmazon

  • 底层交易和履约平台。The underlying transaction and fulfillment platform.
  • 产品、店铺、库存、活动均需符合 Amazon 规则。Products, stores, inventory, and promotions must comply with Amazon rules.

Woot 业务主流程Main Woot Business Process

从客户开发到客户回访，是一条完整的业务闭环。From customer development to follow-up, the process forms a full business loop.

1

客户开发Customer Development

寻找有 Amazon 产品和库存的卖家，判断是否适合做 Woot / Deal。Find sellers with Amazon products and inventory; assess deal fit.

2

产品提报Product Submission

卖家提交 ASIN、库存、价格、FNSKU、店铺等信息。Seller submits ASIN, inventory, price, FNSKU, store, and related information.

3

报价与价格沟通Quotation & Price Communication

核算 Woot 可接受价格，KP 与卖家沟通是否接受。Estimate acceptable Woot price; KP confirms seller acceptance.

4

协议与资料确认Agreement & Materials

完成店铺验证、协议签署、FNSKU / 库存 / 发货方式确认。Verify store, sign agreements, and confirm FNSKU, inventory, and fulfillment mode.

5

提交 WootSubmit to Woot

内部确认后由 CT/CS 提交给 Woot 审核。After internal confirmation, CT/CS submits the case to Woot for review.

6

划库存Inventory Transfer

常规为 FBA 库存划转至 Woot。Usually transfers FBA inventory to Woot.

7

上活动Deal Launch

设置并执行 Woot / Amazon Deal。Set up and execute Woot / Amazon Deal events.

8

广告协助Ad Support

配合广告投放，关注预算、花费、销售额、ACOS、CR、CPC。Support ad operations and monitor budget, spend, sales, ACOS, CR, and CPC.

9

活动中监控In-event Monitoring

检查销量、库存、购物车、价格异常。Monitor sales, inventory, Buy Box, and price issues during the deal.

10

退库存Inventory Return

活动结束或不再继续活动后提交退库存。Submit inventory return after the activity ends or will not continue.

11

效果分析Effect Analysis

分析销量、流量、广告、关键词、库存变化。Analyze sales, traffic, ads, keywords, and inventory changes.

12

结款与返款Settlement & Refund

按库存、退货、预留金、账单多退少补。Settle based on inventory, returns, reserves, and final bills.

13

客户回访Customer Follow-up

总结活动效果，推动继续提报和复购。Review results and drive future submissions and repeat cooperation.

公司内部团队职责Internal Team Responsibilities

Woot 业务是跨部门协作闭环，不是某个部门单独完成。Woot business is a cross-team loop, not a task owned by one single team.

1

OM：客户开发与客户管理OM: Customer Development & Management

  • 开发客户、建立客户编号、维护客户标签。Develop customers, create customer IDs, and maintain customer tags.
  • 同步客户信息，活动后配合回访和复购。Synchronize customer information and support follow-up and repeat cooperation after deals.
2

KP：客户 / 卖家一线接口KP: Frontline Seller Interface

  • 收集产品资料，沟通价格和合作条件。Collect product information and communicate price and cooperation terms.
  • 跟进验证、协议、反馈、退库、发票、收款等事项。Follow up on verification, agreements, feedback, returns, invoices, and payment information.
3

CT / CS：流程推进中枢CT / CS: Process Coordination Hub

  • 整理并提交资料给 Woot，推进订单提交、取消、执行。Prepare and submit materials to Woot; drive order submission, cancellation, and execution.
  • 跟进报价、PORF、PO、划库存、Deal、广告、退库存。Follow up on quotation, PORF, PO, inventory transfer, deals, ads, and returns.
4

MC：订单创建MC: Order Creation

  • 产品导入后在 TBN 创建订单并生成订单号。Create orders in TBN and generate order numbers after product import.
  • 确保订单进入后续审价和执行流程。Ensure orders enter the next review and execution workflow.
5

TAK：广告与效果分析TAK: Advertising & Performance Analysis

  • 提供广告建议，协助活动期广告创建和调整。Provide ad recommendations and support ad creation or adjustments during deals.
  • 分析广告、销售、ACOS、CR、CPC，并判断是否继续提报。Analyze ad metrics, sales, ACOS, CR, and CPC and assess whether to continue submission.
6

财务：结款与返款Finance: Settlement & Refunds

  • 处理返款账单、Seller PI、Seller Word、Invoice。Handle refund bills, Seller PI, Seller Word, and invoices.
  • 安排返款，处理预留金、多退少补和垫资风险。Arrange payments and manage reserves, final adjustments, and advance-funding risk.
7

Woot 对接人 / Woot AMWoot Contacts / Woot AM

  • 通过 Jerry / Sarah / Brad 等中间人对接。Coordinate through Jerry, Sarah, Brad, and other intermediaries.
  • Woot AM 处理审核、报价、划库存、活动执行、退库存等事项。Woot AM handles review, pricing, inventory transfer, deal execution, and returns.
8

优化技术团队：AI / IT / BMOOptimization Technology Team: AI / IT / BMO

  • 支持系统优化、报表优化、流程优化、工具 / 产品改进。Supports system optimization, reporting optimization, process optimization, and tool/product improvements.
  • 提升业务效率、数据沉淀和用户体验。Improves efficiency, data accumulation, and user experience.

核心风险与责任边界Key Risks & Responsibility Boundaries

Woot 业务既要推动销售增长，也要控制价格、库存、结算与合规风险。Woot business must grow sales while controlling price, inventory, settlement, and compliance risks.

1

卖家承担Seller Responsibilities

  • 产品质量、知识产权、Amazon 合规责任。Product quality, intellectual property, and Amazon compliance.
  • 物流与发货责任，产品缺陷、召回、索赔、税务等责任。Logistics, fulfillment, defects, recalls, claims, taxes, and related liabilities.
2

Frual 承担Frual Responsibilities

  • Woot 项目服务、系统流程推进、规则与资金流程管理。Woot project service, process coordination, rules, and funding workflow management.
  • 不承担产品质量、销售额、Amazon 合规等责任。Does not assume responsibility for product quality, sales amount, or Amazon compliance.
3

重点风险Key Risks

  • FBM 自发货风险。FBM seller-fulfilled risk.
  • 退货结算风险与垫资风险。Return/settlement risk and advance-funding risk.
  • 价格库存异常与活动执行异常。Price/inventory anomalies and deal execution issues.

业务关键点Key Takeaways

用于快速记忆的核心结论。Core conclusions for quick memorization.

1

Frual 是服务方，不是货主。Frual is a service provider, not the product owner.

2

系统里建了，不代表 Woot 已经执行。Being created in the system does not mean Woot has executed it.

3

Deal 或广告创建后，需 CT/CS 提交 Woot。After a deal or ad is created, CT/CS must submit it to Woot.

4

结款不能只看销量，还要看库存、退货、账期和预留金。Settlement depends on inventory, returns, billing cycle, and reserves—not only sales.

5

Woot 业务底层逻辑是销售增长与风险控制并重。Woot business balances sales growth with risk control.

6

这是跨部门协作闭环，不是单个部门可以独立完成。It is a cross-team workflow loop, not a task one team can complete alone.
