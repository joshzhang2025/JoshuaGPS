TX 重组方案视频TX Restructuring Plan

1核心判断Core Message

  • AI 不只是工具变化，而是工作方式、组织方式和管理方式的整体升级AI is not merely a tool upgrade; it is a transformation of how we work, organize, and manage.
  • 从 7 月开始，以 AI 为基础、以业务为中心推进 TX 重构Starting in July, the company will advance TX restructuring with AI as the foundation and business as the center.
  • IT → BIT；BMO → ABO；AIX 继续作为方法中心IT → BIT; BMO → ABO; AIX remains the AI methodology center.
  • BU + TU 扁平化管理；GPS + Owner + 跟进小组形成闭环BU + TU flat management; GPS + project owners + follow-up teams create a closed loop.

2为什么重构Why Restructure

  • 传统模式：业务提需求 → BMO 翻译 → IT 开发 → 业务测试Traditional model: business request → BMO translation → IT development → business testing.
  • AI 已能辅助理解流程、整理文档、分析数据、生成方案、写代码、测试和自动化AI can now support process understanding, documentation, data analysis, solution design, coding, testing, and automation.
  • 原有边界导致信息失真、响应慢、责任分散Old boundaries can distort information, slow response, and dilute ownership.
  • 目标：技术更靠近业务，业务更主动使用 AI，管理更透明、扁平、结果导向Goal: bring technology closer to business, make business more proactive with AI, and make management transparent, flat, and results-driven.

3TX 总方向Overall TX Direction

  • 技术转型与业务融合体系TX = Technology Transformation
  • 不是狭义的新部门，而是一种融合技术、AI 与业务优化能力的新工作方式TX is not simply a new department, but a new way of working that integrates technology, AI, and business optimization.
  • 围绕业务问题组队，围绕结果推进Build teams around business problems and drive execution around outcomes.

两类核心角色Two Core Roles

  • BIT：重系统 / 重流程 / 重技术BIT: system-, process-, and technology-intensive work.
  • ABO：重业务 / 轻技术 / AI 可快速解决ABO: business-heavy, light-technology problems that AI can solve quickly.

4BIT：IT 的升级BIT: The Evolution of IT

  • 具备业务理解能力的 ITBIT = Business + IT

角色变化Role Shift

  • 从“接收整理好的需求并开发”From “developing based on pre-defined requirements”
  • 到“直接理解业务、设计方案、开发测试上线，并对业务结果负责”To “understanding the business directly, designing solutions, developing, testing, launching, and owning business outcomes.”

核心职责Key Responsibilities

  • 直接参加业务讨论，理解流程、痛点和业务后果Join business discussions directly and understand workflows, pain points, and business impact.
  • 使用 UMA、Codex、智能问数等工具拆规则、找路径Use UMA, Codex, and intelligent data-query tools to analyze rules and identify technical paths.
  • 明确任务、Owner、时间、测试点和完成标准Define tasks, owners, timelines, test points, and completion criteria.
  • 负责开发、数据处理、接口调整、测试和上线Handle development, data processing, integrations, testing, and deployment.
  • 以减少重复、降低错误、提升效率为结果标准Measure success by reduced repetition, fewer errors, and higher efficiency.

适用场景Best-Fit Scenarios

  • 复杂业务流程、工单系统、账务与复杂数据关系Complex workflows, ticketing systems, accounting, and complex data relationships.
  • 权限、接口、数据流、底层逻辑和老系统重构Permissions, APIs, data flows, system logic, and legacy-system restructuring.

5ABO：BMO 的升级ABO: The Evolution of BMO

  • 用 AI 直接解决业务问题的业务 AI 负责人ABO = AI Business Owner

角色变化Role Shift

  • 从“需求翻译、写文档、对接 IT、跟进进度”From “translating requirements, writing documents, coordinating with IT, and tracking progress”
  • 到“主动发现 AI 场景并直接做出解决方案”To “proactively identifying AI opportunities and building solutions directly.”

核心职责Key Responsibilities

  • 发现手工查询、重复汇总、盯群、催办等场景Identify manual searches, repetitive summaries, message monitoring, and follow-up tasks.
  • 用 AI / Agent / 智能问数 / 看板 / 自动化直接解决Use AI, agents, intelligent queries, dashboards, and automation to solve them directly.
  • 优先解决轻技术、重业务的问题Focus on business-heavy problems that require limited technical development.
  • 与业务共同验证：节省时间、减少错误、提高响应、能否复制Validate with business teams: time saved, errors reduced, response improved, and scalability.

评价方式How ABO Is Evaluated

  • Agent 数量、智能问数应用、AI 化流程Number of agents, intelligent-query applications, and AI-enabled processes.
  • 节省工时、减少重复工作、形成可复制 SOPLabor hours saved, repetitive work reduced, and reusable SOPs created.
  • 实际使用率与结果闭环Actual adoption and closed-loop results.

6AIX 定位Role of AIX

  • 继续作为 AI 突击队、样板房和方法中心Continue as the AI task force, pilot lab, and methodology center.
  • 探索新工具、快速突破、沉淀方法Explore new tools, achieve rapid breakthroughs, and codify methods.
  • 输出 Agent、Codex、智能问数、自动化和业务 AI 化样板Produce model cases for agents, Codex, intelligent queries, automation, and AI-enabled business workflows.
  • 向 BIT、ABO 和业务组复制推广Transfer and scale proven methods to BIT, ABO, and business teams.
  • 保持突击能力，避免被日常小事拖住Preserve rapid-response capability and avoid being consumed by routine tasks.

7管理原则与组织方式Management Principles & Structure

业务在前Business First

  • 提升效率、减少重复和错误Improve efficiency and reduce repetitive work and errors.
  • 提升客户响应、GMV 和利润Improve customer response, GMV, and profitability.
  • 降低风险、提高整体运营效率Reduce risk and improve overall operating efficiency.

BU + TU 扁平化管理BU + TU Flat Management

  • BU：确定业务目标、优先级、业务结果和验收标准BU: define business goals, priorities, expected results, and acceptance criteria.
  • TU：判断技术方向、资源安排、BIT / ABO / AIX 分工TU: determine technical direction, resource allocation, and division of work among BIT, ABO, and AIX.
  • TX：把问题落成项目、Owner、任务、时间、结果和复盘TX: turn problems into projects, owners, tasks, timelines, results, and reviews.

8GPS 管理方法GPS Management Method

目标G — Goal

  • 要解决什么业务问题？为什么要做？完成后有什么变化？What business problem are we solving, why does it matter, and what will change after completion?

优先级P — Priority

  • P0 / P1 / P2；是否外部硬任务；是否影响业务结果P0 / P1 / P2; whether it is externally required; whether it affects business outcomes.

计划与步骤S — Schedule / Step

  • 谁负责？何时完成？每天 / 每周进度？完成标准？Who owns it, when will it be completed, what are the daily/weekly milestones, and what defines completion?
  • 没有时间、Owner 和结果标准，就不算真正拆清楚A task is not properly defined without a timeline, owner, and measurable outcome.

9项目推进机制Project Execution Mechanism

项目 OwnerProject Owner

  • 明确目标、推动项目、协调资源、暴露卡点、对最终结果负责Clarify goals, drive execution, coordinate resources, surface blockers, and own the final result.
  • Owner 不是记录员，而是结果负责人The owner is not a note-taker, but the person accountable for results.

跟进小组Follow-up Team

  • 拆任务：明确工作内容、顺序、周目标、月目标和节点Task planning: define work, sequence, weekly/monthly targets, and milestones.
  • 盯过程：检查推进、文档、卡点、会后行动和计划执行Process tracking: check progress, documentation, blockers, follow-up actions, and plan execution.
  • 盯结果：确认验收、问题解决、沉淀与复制Result tracking: confirm acceptance, problem resolution, documentation, and scalability.

10结果如何呈现How Results Will Be Made Visible

  • 项目看板、P0 任务清单、周度结果榜Project dashboards, P0 task lists, and weekly results boards.
  • AI 应用案例榜、Agent 产出榜AI application case boards and agent output boards.
  • BIT 问题解决榜、ABO AI 应用榜BIT problem-solving boards and ABO AI application boards.
  • 效率提升案例、MTA / SOP 沉淀清单Efficiency improvement cases and MTA/SOP documentation lists.
  • 目的：看见成果、识别有效方法、复制经验、暴露卡点Purpose: make outcomes visible, identify effective methods, replicate experience, and expose blockers.

11各角色变化总结Summary of Role Changes

  • IT → BIT：从技术执行者升级为业务结果导向的技术负责人IT → BIT: from technical executor to business-outcome-oriented technical owner.
  • BMO → ABO：从需求翻译者升级为 AI 业务解决者BMO → ABO: from requirements translator to AI-powered business problem solver.
  • AIX：保持突击能力并成为方法输出中心AIX: retain task-force capability and become the methodology output center.
  • 业务组：从提需求、等支持，转为主动参与 AI 化和验收Business teams: move from submitting requests and waiting for support to actively participating in AI adoption and acceptance.
  • BU：更深度参与优先级、结果和跨组复制BU: take a deeper role in prioritization, outcomes, and cross-team replication.
  • TU：负责技术方向、AI 方法、系统底座和资源协调TU: own technical direction, AI methods, platform foundations, and resource coordination.

  • 建立跟进小组和结果沉淀机制Establish follow-up teams and result-documentation mechanisms.

13对全体同事的要求Expectations for Everyone

  • 每个人都要学会使用 AIEveryone must learn to use AI.
  • 每个业务问题先思考能否 AI 化For every business problem, first consider whether AI can help.
  • 每个项目必须结果导向Every project must be outcome-oriented.
  • 每个团队都要参与组织 AI 化Every team must participate in the company’s AI transformation.
  • 业务懂场景，技术懂系统，AIX 懂方法，ABO 懂应用，BIT 懂实现Business understands scenarios; technology understands systems; AIX understands methods; ABO understands applications; BIT understands implementation.

14最终结论Final Conclusion

  • 以 AI 为基础AI as the foundation
  • 以业务为中心Business at the center
  • 以 TX 为组织载体TX as the organizational framework
  • 以 BIT 和 ABO 为角色升级BIT and ABO as upgraded roles
  • 以 AIX 为方法中心AIX as the methodology center
  • 以 BU + TU 推动扁平化管理BU + TU for flat management
  • 以 GPS、Owner、跟进小组形成闭环GPS, owners, and follow-up teams for closed-loop execution
