In-House Services

Six Decoration Methods, One Supplier

Every decoration method handled in-house — no outsourcing, no delays, consistent quality across every order.

Embroidery

Classic, durable thread-based decoration for headwear, apparel, and accessories. Ideal for structured logos and detailed artwork.

DTF / Heat Transfer

Full-color, photographic-quality prints on virtually any fabric. No minimums on most items; perfect for vibrant, edge-to-edge designs.

Patches

Woven, embroidered, or PVC patches that can be sewn or iron-on applied. A premium, collectible decoration format.

Screen Print

High-volume, cost-effective spot-color printing on textiles. Best for bold graphics and large runs with consistent color reproduction.

Sublimation

Dye-sublimation for all-over prints on polyester and hard goods. Produces vibrant, permanent color with no hand-feel on the fabric.

Laser Etching

Precision laser engraving on leather, wood, metal, and fabric. A premium, tactile decoration method suited for executive gifts and specialty items.
