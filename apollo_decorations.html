DTF / Heat Transfer

In-House Service · Method 03

Patches

Patch twill, woven, and leather etched patches are used to create a premium luxurious look to your logo. Great application for bags, premium blankets, or a wide range of products you are looking to elevate.

In-House Service · Method 04

Screen Print

A printing technique that uses a woven mesh to support an ink-blocking stencil. Nylon inks are used for fabrics with a high nylon content or stretch factor. Plastisol inks are used on all cotton-rich products. Ideal for various fabrics and large-format applications at a low cost.

In-House Service · Method 05

Sublimation

Sublimation permanently adds a design to a sublimation blank — shirts, ornaments, tumblers, and more — creating an amazing variety of custom products. Produces vibrant, permanent color with no hand-feel on the fabric.

Request a Quote
In-House Service · Method 06

Laser Etching

Laser etching creates marks on parts and products by melting their surface, producing raised marks in black, white, or gray. Highly versatile, it works with metals, leather, wood, and fabric — a premium tactile decoration method suited for executive gifts and specialty items.
