Project Overview · PromoProofAI Mockup项目概览 · PromoProofAI Mockup

See the logo on the product — before it's ever made.在生产之前，先看到 Logo 在产品上的样子。

An AI tool where a customer uploads their logo and instantly gets a realistic preview — embroidered, screen-printed, or DTF on the actual Apollo product, plus a model wearing it. Here's what it does, the problem it solves, and why it matters.一个 AI 工具：客户上传 Logo，立刻得到逼真的预览 —— 在真实的 Apollo 产品上呈现刺绣、丝印或 DTF 效果，还能配上模特穿着图。下面讲清楚它做什么、解决什么问题、为什么重要。

01

What it does它做什么

From a logo to a customer-ready proof, in minutes从一个 Logo 到客户可看的打样，只需几分钟

The whole tool is one short flow. The customer or sales rep brings a product and a logo; the tool does the rest and hands back something a customer can actually understand and approve.整个工具就是一条短流程。客户或销售带来产品和 Logo，工具完成其余步骤，交回一份客户真正看得懂、能确认的东西。

01
Upload product + logo上传产品 + Logo
Pick an Apollo product, drop in the customer's logo.选一个 Apollo 产品，放入客户的 Logo。
02
Generate the mockup生成 Mockup
AI renders it as it'll really look — embroidery, DTF, or screen print.AI 还原真实呈现 —— 刺绣、DTF 或丝印。
03
Add a model shot加上模特图
Show the product worn, in a lifestyle scene.展示产品穿着效果与场景图。
04
Send the proof发送打样
Generate a proof sheet to send for sign-off.生成打样表，发出去确认。

The point is the decoration accuracy — not a flat logo pasted on a photo, but how the thread or ink will actually sit on the garment.重点是工艺真实度 —— 不是把平面 Logo 贴到照片上，而是还原针线或油墨在衣物上的真实呈现。

02

The problem it solves它解决的问题

Why the current way is too slow为什么现在的方式太慢

Today, when someone wants to see a logo on an Apollo product, the options are slow: wait days for a physical sample, or get a rough Photoshop comp from the team. Time is what kills deals in this industry — a slow quote loses to a faster competitor even when Apollo's pricing and quality are better.如今，当有人想看到 Logo 在 Apollo 产品上的样子时，选择都很慢：等几天做实体样品，或让团队做个粗糙的 Photoshop 拼图。在这个行业，时间决定成败 —— 报价慢就会输给更快的对手，哪怕 Apollo 的价格和质量更好。

Apollo
Sales spends hours per quote manually mocking up logos, or waits days for a physical sample — capacity that could close more deals.销售每单要花数小时手动做 Logo 效果图，或等几天做实体样品 —— 这些精力本可用来成更多单。
Distributor分销商
Needs to show their client something convincing, fast — often before the client has committed. Slow turnaround pushes them to a faster supplier.需要快速给他们的客户拿出有说服力的东西 —— 往往在客户还没下决心之前。周转慢就会把他们推向更快的供应商。
End customer最终客户
Can't picture an embroidered logo from a flat vector file. Without a visual, it's hard to approve — so the order stalls.仅凭一个平面矢量文件，想象不出刺绣后的样子。没有可视化就难以确认 —— 订单就此卡住。

The real problem isn't "we need nicer pictures." It's that the gap between "customer has a logo" and "customer approves an order" is too slow, and today it's filled by manual labor instead of software.真正的问题不是"我们需要更好看的图"。而是从"客户有了 Logo"到"客户确认下单"之间这段太慢，而如今填补它的是人工，不是软件。

03

Why it matters为什么重要

What Apollo actually gainsApollo 真正得到什么

Faster deal velocity更快的成交速度

A proof that used to take days comes back in minutes, so the customer decides while they're still interested.原本要几天的打样几分钟就能返回，客户还在兴头上就能拍板。

Higher conversion更高的转化率

People approve what they can see. A clear, accurate mockup turns more quotes into orders.人们会确认看得见的东西。清晰、准确的 Mockup 能把更多报价变成订单。

Freed-up sales time解放销售时间

Sales stops doing manual Photoshop work and spends that time selling instead.销售不再做手动 Photoshop，把时间花在真正的销售上。

In one line: this isn't about generating pretty images — it's about helping Apollo's sales and distributors get customers to understand and approve faster, shortening the path from quote to order.一句话：这不是为了生成漂亮的图 —— 而是帮 Apollo 的销售和分销商让客户更快看懂并确认，缩短从报价到下单的路径。

04

The bottom line结论

Worth solving — the how comes next值得解决 —— 怎么做是下一步

Is the problem real?问题真实吗？ Yes.真实。

Slow proofing genuinely costs deals for Apollo, its distributors, and end customers alike. Anything that shortens "logo → approved order" has clear value.打样慢确实让 Apollo、分销商和最终客户都丢单。任何能缩短"Logo → 确认下单"的东西都有明确价值。

Is it worth doing?值得做吗？ Yes — if built smart.值得 —— 但要聪明地做。

The value is in faster, decoration-accurate proofs tied to Apollo's own workflow — not in pretty images for their own sake.价值在于更快、工艺准确、并与 Apollo 自身流程绑定的打样 —— 而不是为了好看而生成图。

Build or buy?自建还是采购？ Depends on the market.取决于市场。

That call rests on what already exists. We mapped the full landscape — which tools fit, what's already solved, and what's still missing — in the competitor research.这取决于市场上已有什么。我们在竞品调研里完整梳理了全局 —— 哪些工具契合、哪些已解决、还缺什么。

VERDICT

Build, buy, or skip?自建、采购，还是放弃？

What the research above adds up to, and what to do next.上面调研的结论，以及下一步该做什么。

Build from scratch?从零自建？ No.不。

The space isn't empty — industry tools like Merch.ai already do much of this, and generic tools cover the basics for free. The one thing nobody fully nails is decoration-method realism. That's the wedge.这个领域并不空白 —— Merch.ai 等行业工具已做了大部分，通用工具也免费覆盖基础需求。唯一没人真正做好的是工艺真实度。那就是切入点。

So what?那怎么办？ Integrate first.先整合。

Trial the best existing tool on real Apollo products before writing generation code. Build a thin Apollo-branded layer on top — only build custom if the realism gap proves out in practice.先用真实 Apollo 产品试用最好的现成工具，再写生成代码。在其上做一层薄薄的 Apollo 品牌外壳 —— 只有当工艺差距在实测中确实存在时，才自研。
