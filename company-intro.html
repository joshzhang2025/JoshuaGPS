1一、给新同事的第一句话：先看全局，再做具体工作The First Message to New Colleagues: See the Big Picture First, Then Do the Work

新同事进入公司后，最容易先看到的是自己岗位上的具体任务：开会、跟进、做表、处理订单、整理资料、对接客户或系统。但如果只看单点任务，就很难理解为什么公司这么强调会议记录、部门隔离、系统流程、Code、TMM、PEA、AI、周计划和复盘。When new colleagues join, the first thing they tend to see is the specific tasks of their own role: attending meetings, following up, making spreadsheets, processing orders, organizing materials, and dealing with customers or systems. But if you only look at single-point tasks, it is hard to understand why the company places such emphasis on meeting records, department isolation, system processes, Codes, TMM, PEA, AI, weekly plans, and retrospectives.

所以，公司介绍培训的第一目标，是让新同事先建立"全局地图"：公司从哪里来，为什么形成现在的组织和工作方式；公司现在有哪些主要业务；中国团队和美国团队如何协作；新同事应该如何把自己的岗位放到整个业务链条里理解。So the first goal of this company-introduction training is to help new colleagues build a "global map" first: where the company came from and why it formed its current organization and ways of working; what main businesses it has now; how the China and U.S. teams collaborate; and how a new colleague should understand their own role within the entire business chain.

这份材料整合了旧版公司发展历史、主营业务介绍，以及最新的 Frual 巧豚豚集团介绍、美国公司介绍原稿，供新同事作为入职前两周的基础学习资料。This material integrates the older company history and main-business introduction with the latest Frual / Qiaotuntun Group introduction and the original U.S. company introduction, serving as foundational study material for new colleagues during their first two weeks.

2二、公司整体定位：横跨中美的跨境电商综合型技术型商业集团Overall Positioning: A China–US Cross-Border E-Commerce, Comprehensive and Technology-Driven Business Group

Frual / 巧豚豚集团可以理解为一个横跨中美、以跨境电商为核心的综合型商业集团。它不是只做一个平台，也不是只做单一代运营，而是整合了美国本土资源、中国卖家资源、平台服务能力、品牌产品能力、内容与流量能力。The Frual / Qiaotuntun Group can be understood as a China–US, cross-border-e-commerce-centered, comprehensive business group. 公司当前的核心价值，是把"美国资源 + 中国运营 + 平台能力 + 产品品牌 + 系统工具"整合起来，为品牌和卖家提供从产品到销售、从渠道到增长的全链路解决方案。The company's current core value is integrating "U.S. resources + China operations + platform capabilities + product brands + system tools" to provide brands and sellers with full-chain solutions from product to sales and from channel to growth.

3三、Paul：技术驱动业务的一号位Paul: The Number-One Leader Driving Business Through Technology

Paul 是公司长期发展的核心推动者，也是一位技术型企业家。曾在美国华尔街 Morgan Stanley 美国金融与技术机构工作十年，从事市场数据、系统开发和技术管理工作，具备扎实的 IT 技术基础、系统思维和解决复杂问题的能力。Paul is the core driver of the company's long-term development and a technology-minded entrepreneur. He spent ten years at Morgan Stanley on Wall Street and in U.S. financial and technology institutions, working on market data, system development, and technical management, giving him a solid IT foundation, systems thinking, and the ability to solve complex problems.

Paul 最突出的特点，是善于把技术应用到真实业务中。公司早期绣花打版业务，就是在他的推动下，通过电子化、网络化方式改造传统流程，从而建立效率优势。此后，公司从打版业务逐步拓展到 B2B、B2C、电商运营、Amazon / Woot 服务、保健品品牌和系统化运营，也一直延续着"用技术提升业务效率"的发展逻辑。Paul's most distinctive trait is his skill in applying technology to real business. The company's early embroidery-digitizing business was, under his push, transformed through digitization and networking of traditional processes, building an efficiency advantage. The company then expanded from digitizing into B2B, B2C, e-commerce operations, Amazon / Woot services, supplement brands, and systematized operations, consistently following the logic of "using technology to improve business efficiency."

进入 AI 时代后，Paul 作为公司一号位，正在带领公司从传统系统化运营进一步走向 AI 化。他强调技术要服务业务，IT 要理解业务，业务也要学习 AI，用 AI 和自动化工具重塑工作流程、提升效率、沉淀组织能力。Paul 的风格低调务实，更关注技术是否真正解决问题、创造结果和形成长期价值。In the AI era, Paul, as the company's number-one leader, is guiding the company from traditional systematized operations toward AI adoption. He emphasizes that technology must serve the business, IT must understand the business, and the business must also learn AI — using AI and automation tools to reshape workflows, improve efficiency, and accumulate organizational capability. Paul's style is low-key and pragmatic, focusing on whether technology truly solves problems, creates results, and forms long-term value.
